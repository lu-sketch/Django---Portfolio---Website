My journey


My journey started on a livestock and game farm. Outside every day with the animals, it was heaven! Spent as much of my time as possible outside.

My journey continued, after school, studying as an Agricultural Economist. I embarked on my Agricultural Economic journey with the Department of Agriculture in Bloemfontein. There I did market research. We held farmer days to help upcoming farmers with step-by-step instructions on how to identify their target markets and how to deliver their products to their customers. I learned how to do project feasibility studies, project management for upcoming farmer's projects. We held market research workshops to educate and advise farmers about the marketing chain and how to deliver their products to their markets/customers fast and efficiently.

After life happened, it took me on a different path, one that led to Data Science and Hyperiondev. I learned my first programming language -- Python. Then I moved on to Machine learning and Deep Learning. The biggest surprise for me was....I love this!!
